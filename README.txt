Double-click on a file to open it.

Click-and-drag a track to move it in the time dimension; click-and-drag on empty screen to pan the view.

Hit spacebar to start and stop.

Use the left arrow and right arrow keys to move backwards and forwards 4 bars at a time.

***

'yadjc.py' is the entry point. 'yadjc-old.py' was the entry point for the first two demos, kept for historical interest. Sorry everything is a little messy...
